About Me

Hi, I’m Payam — a flight simulation enthusiast and virtual student pilot documenting my journey in aviation. This website serves as my personal flight logbook, where I record my flights, routes, and aircraft experiences. I’m continuously learning, and I share each flight as part of that process. You may notice both minor and major mistakes along the way — they’re simply part of learning. :)

Through simulation, I explore realistic airline operations, navigation, and procedures.

About This Project

This logbook is a collection of my virtual flights, presented through an interactive route map. Each route represents a completed flight, including dedicated pages with videos, screenshots, and flight information.
